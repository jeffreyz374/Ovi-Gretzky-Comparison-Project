Miscellaneous Interactive Visuals

Xiaotang (Jeffrey) Zhou


These are additional interactive visuals that are derived from the information on all of the goals ever scored by Ovechkin and Gretzky. Although not relevant to the scope of the project, these visuals still provide a comparison between the individual goal scoring talents of both players.

Note that as Ovechkin is currently an active player in the NHL, any data corresponding to him is accurate as of the last update to this website. This update was most recently performed on March 21st 2025.



Ovechkin and Gretzky Goals By Season

This interactive visual shows the number of goals scored by Ovechkin and Gretzky grouped by the NHL season in which each player scored those goals.

Note that as Ovechkin is currently an active player in the NHL, any data corresponding to him is accurate as of the last update to this website. This update was most recently performed on March 21st 2025.

Ovechkin and Gretzky Goals By Age

This interactive visual shows the number of goals scored by Ovechkin and Gretzky grouped by the age at which each player scored those goals.

As Ovechkin was born on September 17th 1985, the visual corresponding to him will be identical to the previous one depicting the frequency of his goals grouped by NHL season, since September is not within the NHL season.

In contrast, as Gretzky was born on January 26th 1961, the visual corresponding to him is different from the previous one depicting the frequency of his goals grouped by NHL season, since January is in the middle of the NHL season.

Note that as Ovechkin is currently an active player in the NHL, any data corresponding to him is accurate as of the last update to this website. This update was most recently performed on March 21st 2025.
